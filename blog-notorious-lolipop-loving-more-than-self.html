Notorious Lolipop - Loving more than self

Every morning the four legged furball begged all of us to stay home. And now he is surprised that that after so many months and years the hoomans listened to him. He is so happy to have all of us around him. He would nudge his nose to mommy’s feet while she is busy with work or happy to pop up in our video calls of school learning.
